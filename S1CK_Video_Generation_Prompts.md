## Video 1: Hero Lifestyle Video (10–12 seconds)

**Purpose:** Background video for above-the-fold hero section. Sets the S1CK brand world – luxury, confidence, attraction.

**Visual Style:** Cinematic, high-end, aspirational. Shot on RED or Alexa. Color grading with warm blacks, deep shadows, gold accent lighting. Think high-end fragrance advertising (Creed, Tom Ford, Dior).

**Detailed Prompt:**

```
Create a cinematic 12-second luxury lifestyle video for a high-end pheromone fragrance brand (S1CK). 

OPENING SCENE (0–3 seconds):
- Wide shot of a sophisticated man in a luxury penthouse or upscale lounge
- Sitting at a sleek bar with dim, warm lighting
- He reaches for a bottle (not on screen yet) with confident, deliberate movement
- Camera slowly dolly-in on his face, confidence in his eyes
- Subtle jazz music begins (instrumental, sophisticated)

MID SECTION (3–7 seconds):
- Quick cuts (1–2 seconds each) showing moments of connection:
  * Man and woman making eye contact across a room at an upscale event
  * Close-up of a woman leaning in close, intrigued smile
  * Man confidently talking to a group, people drawn to him
  * Subtle spray motion of fragrance mist in golden light (slow-motion preferred)
  * Woman's expression shifting from curiosity to attraction
- Each cut should feel effortless and powerful
- Golden accent lighting on faces, emphasizing confidence
- Background should be upscale venues: rooftop bars, lounges, upscale gatherings

CLOSING SCENE (7–12 seconds):
- Return to original man in penthouse, now with a subtle, confident smile
- Camera pulls back slowly as he looks out at the city lights below
- Fragrance bottle appears in foreground for last 1–2 seconds (bottle design: elegant, crystalline cap, dark liquid)
- Final frame: Bottle with subtle glow, fade to black
- Text appears: "S1CK – Scientifically Formulated to Attract"

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
- Resolution: 1920×1080 (Full HD)
- Frame rate: 24fps (cinematic feel)
- Color grading: Warm blacks, gold highlights, deep shadows, high contrast
- Lighting: Moody, aspirational, luxury hotel/lounge aesthetic
- Music: Instrumental jazz, sophisticated, 2–3 minute track
- Tempo: Slow, confident, no fast cuts (builds intrigue)
- Mood: Confidence, luxury, subtle sensuality, effortless attraction
- No dialogue or voiceover
- No logo/branding until final 1–2 seconds

CAMERA MOVEMENTS:
- Slow dolly-in for intimate moments
- Smooth pans revealing new scenes
- Tracking shots through spaces
- Slow pull-backs for transitions
- Parallax effect between foreground and background

EXTRAS/TALENT:
- 1 male lead (30–45 years old, confident presence)
- 2–3 female extras (natural, elegant)
- Diverse ethnicities encouraged
- Professional actors or models
```
